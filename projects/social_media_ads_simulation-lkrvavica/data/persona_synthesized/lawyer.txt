Rami Amaro is a trial lawyer with more than 18 years of experience in the areas of Aviation, Corporate, Employment and Insurance Law. While enrolled in law school, Rami served as an intern for the Federal District Court in Northern Kentucky and as a Legislative Research Intern for the Kentucky Municipality Association. Upon graduation from law school, she relocated to Coeur d’Alene, Idaho, where she worked as an Associate Attorney at Ramsden & Lyons. In 2000, she joined Johnson Law Group, but soon founded the Amaro Law Office, at which she served as managing partner until 2014. During her tenure at the Amaro Law Office she consistently demonstrated that she is a dedicated attorney who works tirelessly on behalf of her clients. During her career spanning almost two decades, Rami Amaro has held a myriad of professional roles including, but not limited to: Pro-Tem Judge for the Kootenai County DUI Court; Special Deputy Attorney General and Administrative Law Judge for the State of Idaho; Legal Instructor for Lorman Education; Arbitrator for NASD/FINRA; and an Idaho Supreme Court certified Mediator. She is licensed to practice law in Idaho, Oregon, Washington, and Ohio. Rami Amaro joined Medflightone LLC in 2014 where she currently serves as the Vice President and General Counsel. Rami has extensive experience and knowledge in business leadership, including but not limited to the areas of human resources, marketing, growth management, startups and more. Rami Amaro has dedicated her life to helping people less fortunate than her. She has engaged in extensive pro bono work, primarily assisting victims of domestic violence as well as fighting their insurance companies for medical coverage for needed treatment. Rami is passionate about “paying it forward”, recognizing that she should do her part to help others. She firmly believes that a large part of one’s life should be dedicated to making the world a better place. For more from Rami, check out her blog.
